Rhombus

All 4 sides of a rhombus are of equal length. Opposite sides are parallel and opposite angles are equal.

Polygon > Quadrilateral > Parallelogram > Rhombus

Square

All 4 sides of a square are of equal length and each angle is a right angle. A square is also a rhombus or a rectangle.

Polygon > Quadrilateral > Parallelogram > Rectangle / Rhombus > Square

Rectangle

A parallelogram with 4 right angles, that are of equal length. The angles are equal where the pairs meet.

Polygon > Quadrilateral > Parallelogram > Rectangle

Rhomboid

A parallelogram that is neither a rhombus (since its adjacent sides are of unequal lengths) nor a rectangle (since its angles are not right angled).

Polygon > Quadrilateral > Parallelogram > Rhomboid

Kite

A kite has two pairs of adjacent sides that are of equal length. The angles are equal where the pairs meet. Sometimes, if a kite meets certain requirements, it could also be a rhombus or a square.

Polygon > Quadrilateral > Kite

Trapezoid

At least one pair of opposite sides is parallel. Some definitions say that only one pair of opposite sides must be parallel.

Polygon > Quadrilateral > Trapezoid

Isosceles Trapezoid

A trapezoid that also has its base angles equal in measure.

Polygon > Quadrilateral > Trapezoid

Triangle: Equilateral

A triangle is a polygon with 3 edges and 3 vertices. When all sides have the same length and all angles measure 60 degrees, it is an equilateral triangle.

Polygon > Triangle

Triangle: Isosceles

A triangle with 2 equal sides; the angles opposite the equal sides are equal.

Polygon > Triangle

Triangle: Scalene

A triangle where all sides and all angles are unequal.

Polygon > Triangle

Triangle: Right

A triangle where one of its interior angles is a right angle.

Polygon > Triangle
